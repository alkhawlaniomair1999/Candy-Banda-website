Company Overview

Candy Panda Global Co., Ltd is a China-based export and sourcing company specializing in confectionery and snack products for international markets. We provide best Chinese products for distributors and brand owners across the Middle East, Africa, and Europe.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, innovative, and market-ready confectionery products by building strong, transparent, and long-term partnerships between China and global markets.

Our Vision

To become a trusted global platform for confectionery exports, known for quality sourcing, regulatory awareness, and professional international trade execution.
